Who we are ?

Story about us

Hello dear friends! We are recently set up VIMC organization, who takes care about research school's within NIS in Semey. My name is Michael and let me tell you the story about what happened, so we decided to create this organization. That was usual day of NIS's student. Me and a friend (colleague) of mine were have to create the project for our GP classes (Global Perspective). We merely had no ideas to come up with. Nothing was on our minds. We even attempted to look for other students' projects, but it went awry (P.S. do not tell it to our teacher). During the conversation, we spoke about our folders with certificates from any competitions and that we have to take part in some olympiads, in order to put some of the diplomas in it, because it plays an incredile role after graduation. Suddenly, we thought about the research schools and that it is pretty much more easier to gain the diploma, by accomplishing research work and presenting your final project; you will be definately awarded right there, at least, because you did something and that seems to be useful, even if someone tells you that it will no longer bring you profit. Tastes different. Where were we? Unfortunately, research schools are not so demanded today and students have to deal with olympiads, where it is barely impracticable to take at least the third place. However, from now (2020) the new epoch of research schools begins and VIMC organization will contribute to it. We are founders of new time; new community; new world. Join to our organization. Because, FUTURE WITH US...
